SIGNAL

SIGNAL is a reality web show which follows the daily lives of tripleS, offering a closer look at their routines and interactions. Being first uploaded on May 9, 2022, with a unique format where episodes were filmed, edited, and uploaded on the same day. However, beginning with Cream01, in October 2023, the format was changed. Episodes are now often pre-filmed with uploads typically scheduled for Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays.
