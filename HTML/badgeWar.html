BADGE WAR

Badge War is a reality show where the members compete in a variety of different missions to win badges. The member with the most badges is declared the winner. The first season, Strong Girl: Badge War, aired from December 7 to December 28, 2023, featuring the first 16 members. The second season aired on May 24, 2024, featuring all 24 members.
