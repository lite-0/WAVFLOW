COSMO

Cosmo: the Gate is the official app for MODHAUS groups, including tripleS, being launched on August 8, 2022. It serves as the central platform where fans can engage with exclusive content, and actively participate in shaping the future of tripleS.

The app's main ability is allowing fans to vote on important decisions, such as song selections, and unit member lineups, through the use of COMO, a digital currency used to vote.

Objekts are digital photocards that can be collected, and traded with other fans. Objekts can be purchased digitally through the app, or in the Cosmo Web Shop, or physically, through album, and merchandise inclusions.
